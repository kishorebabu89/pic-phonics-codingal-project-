How to Play
  1. Look at the letter image.
  2. If you wish to hear the letter sound, press the 'click for letter sound' button.
  3. Then, take a look at the pictures below the letter.
  4. Choose the picture that starts with that letter.
  5. Select your answer by clicking on the picture.
  6. When an answer has been selected, an alert will let you know if it was correct or incorrect.
  7. The words associated with the pictures will then be revealed.
  8. After a few seconds the next question will be displayed.
  9. This process is repeated until all questions have been answered.
  10. Once you have answered all questions you will be given your final result.
  11. You will then be given options to:
    • Generate a certificate with your name and result
    • Restart the Game
    • Exit the Game
  12. Enjoy and have fun!
